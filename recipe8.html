The Correct Grilled Chicken
Ingredients
1 Chicken Breast 1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar
half lemon squeezed 1/2 cup of olive oil
1 teaspoon Italian Herb seasoning 1 teaspoon Onion powder/salt
1 teaspoon Garlic powder Dash of Basil
pinch of cayenne Red pepper flakes to liking
pinch of peprika 1 gallon Ziploc bag
Directions
  • Place chicken in Ziploc Bag
  • add all ingredients into Ziploc bag
  • close Ziploc bag making sure to seal out any air in the bag
  • massage the ingredients around to cover the whole chicken as best as possible
  • place in fridge for 5-10 minutes
  • flip the bag over to the other side and leave in fridge for another 5-10 minutes
  • take out chicken Breast and place on grill
  • Wait until Chicken no longer sticks to the grill and then flip over to the other side
  • wait for some of the juices inside the chicken to come out then leave for 2 more minutes trying to cover both sides to look equally grilled on the outside
    • Grilling one chicken at around 400° should take around 10 minutes but keep in mind overcooking chicken cannot lead to salmonella infection
  • leave uncut for around 5-10 minutes for the chicken to internally cook for a bit longer
    • Wrapping the chicken during this is also a good option
